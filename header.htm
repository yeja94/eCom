ABOUT

Our business is about repair and refurnish used furnitures including antique, household, art and craft items. We try our best and perfect the items as it looks like new or in excellent condition. We care about our customer satisfaction as well as providing our best work for you. In addition, we are always looking for trade or even buy your old furntiures that you don't need anymore. Certain requirements must be met in order for us to trade or buy. Our location is located in South East Portland, Oregon. We do not provide shipping service and we only accept cash and local pick up. Please contact us if you have any question or concern.
